## Choosing a Payment Method

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="E-commerce checkout" icon="cart-shopping">
    **Recommended:** Cards (3DS) + Apple Pay + Google Pay
    
    Offer multiple options to maximize conversion:
    - Cards for traditional checkout
    - Wallets for one-tap mobile payments
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Subscription billing" icon="rotate">
    **Recommended:** Cards with tokenization
    
    Store payment credentials securely for recurring charges without re-entering card details.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="High-value transactions" icon="gem">
    **Recommended:** ACH bank transfer
    
    Lower fees for large amounts; ideal for B2B or high-ticket items.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Mobile-first" icon="mobile">
    **Recommended:** Apple Pay + Google Pay
    
    Fastest checkout experience on mobile devices.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
