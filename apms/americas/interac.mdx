# Interac

<Info>
  **Payment Method ID:** `apmgw_Interac`  
  **Type:** Debit / E-Transfer  
  **Countries:** Canada üá®üá¶  
  **Currencies:** CAD  
  **Market Share:** ~30% of Canadian e-commerce
</Info>

Interac is Canada's national debit network. Online, Interac e-Transfer allows customers to send payments directly from their bank accounts.

## How Interac Works

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant C as Customer
    participant M as Merchant
    participant N as Nuvei
    participant I as Interac
    participant B as Canadian Bank
    
    C->>M: Select Interac
    M->>N: Payment request
    N-->>M: Redirect URL
    M->>C: Redirect to Interac
    C->>I: Select bank
    I->>B: Redirect
    C->>B: Login & authorize
    B->>I: Confirmed
    I->>N: Payment confirmed
    N->>M: Webhook (APPROVED)
```
