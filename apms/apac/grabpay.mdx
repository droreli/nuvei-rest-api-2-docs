GrabPay is Southeast Asia's leading super-app payment wallet, integrated into the Grab ride-hailing and delivery ecosystem with over 200 million users.

## How GrabPay Works

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant C as Customer
    participant M as Merchant
    participant N as Nuvei
    participant G as Grab
    
    C->>M: Select GrabPay
    M->>N: Payment request
    N-->>M: Redirect URL
    M->>C: Redirect to Grab
    C->>G: Approve in Grab app
    G->>N: Payment confirmed
    N->>M: Webhook (APPROVED)
    M->>C: Order confirmation
```
