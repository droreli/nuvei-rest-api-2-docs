POLi is the leading online banking payment method for Australia and New Zealand, enabling real-time bank transfers directly from customer accounts.

## How POLi Works

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant C as Customer
    participant M as Merchant
    participant N as Nuvei
    participant P as POLi
    participant B as Customer's Bank
    
    C->>M: Select POLi
    M->>N: Payment request
    N-->>M: Redirect URL
    M->>C: Redirect to POLi
    C->>P: Select bank
    P->>B: Redirect to bank login
    C->>B: Authenticate & approve
    B->>P: Confirmed
    P->>N: Payment confirmed
    N->>M: Webhook (APPROVED)
```
